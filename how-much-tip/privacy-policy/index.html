Privacy Policy for How Much Tip?

Last updated: January 26, 2024

Introduction

This privacy policy applies to "How Much Tip?", a simple Android application designed to calculate tips for various services such as restaurants, hair salons, and other tipping establishments. This policy outlines our handling of any personal data related to the usage of "How Much Tip?".

Data Collection and Usage

"How Much Tip?" does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data. The app asks users questions about the type of establishment and the quality of service to calculate the tip amount. All data entered by users remains on their device and is not saved locally or to the cloud. The data entered is temporarily stored in memory during the app's use and is discarded when the app is closed.

Contact Information

For any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at contact@ericdudley.com.

Data Handling and Security

Since "How Much Tip?" does not collect, store, or process any personal or sensitive data, there are no specific data handling and security procedures in place. The app operates entirely within the confines of the user's device without transmitting data externally.

Data Retention and Deletion Policy

As "How Much Tip?" does not retain user data, there is no need for a data retention or deletion policy. User data is not saved by the app, ensuring privacy and data security.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

This privacy policy is effective as of January 26, 2024. By using "How Much Tip?", you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.
